3. De beata Maria V.

Versus catenati simpliciter.

Virgo Maria, rosa, species caeli speciosa,
For-mula formosa, restaurans crimine rosa,
In mala spinosa spem spirans, imperiosa,
Vinea vinosa, virtus vitans vitiosa,
